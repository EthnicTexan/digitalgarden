# Republic Of Texas Era
---
The **Republic of Texas era (1836â€“1845)** was a decade of high ambition, crushing debt, and intense political division as the new nation struggled to survive on its own before eventually joining the United States.
##### This era spans from **1836 to 1845**

# 1836
---
> * **The Alamo & Goliad:** In early 1836, Mexican forces under Santa Anna wiped out the defenders at the Alamo and executed prisoners at Goliad. These losses became powerful rallying cries: *"Remember the Alamo!"*
>* **Independence:** On **March 2, 1836**, delegates signed the Texas Declaration of Independence at Washington-on-the-Brazos.
>* On **April 21, 1836**, A captured Santa Anna was forced to sign **The Treaties of Velasco** ending the war and recognizing (unofficially) Texas's independence. The **Republic of Texas** was born, with Sam Houston serving as its first elected president.


# 1837
---
>**Seeking Recognition:** The United States officially recognizes Texas as an independent nation; the new government struggles with massive debt and the "Panic of 1837" economic downturn.

# 1838
---
>**The Lamar Transition:** Mirabeau B. Lamar is elected President; he opposes annexation to the U.S. and envisions Texas as a vast empire stretching to the Pacific.


# 1839
---
> **A New Capital:** The capital is moved from Houston to the frontier site of Austin; Lamar begins an aggressive (and expensive) military campaign to remove the Cherokee from East Texas.



# 1840
---
> **Frontier Warfare:** The Council House Fight in San Antonio leads to the Great Comanche Raid, the largest Native American raid on white settlements in U.S./Texas history.


# 1841
---
>**The Santa Fe Failure:** Lamar sends the Santa Fe Expedition to claim New Mexico, ending in a disastrous surrender; Sam Houston is elected to a second, non-consecutive term to fix the crashing economy.



# 1842
---
>**Mexican Invasions:** Mexican armies twice capture San Antonio; the "Archive War" breaks out in Austin, and the failed Mier Expedition leads to the "Black Bean Affair" executions.


# 1843
---
> **Diplomatic Maneuvering:** Houston negotiates an armistice with Mexico and uses British interest in Texas to "jealous" the United States into reconsidering annexation.


# 1844
---
> **The Annexation Election:** Anson Jones is elected the final President of Texas; in the U.S., James K. Polk wins the presidency on a platform of "Manifest Destiny" and bringing Texas into the Union.



# 1845
---
>**The End of the Republic:** The U.S. Congress passes a joint resolution for annexation; Texas voters approve the deal, and on December 29, Texas officially becomes the 28th state.
