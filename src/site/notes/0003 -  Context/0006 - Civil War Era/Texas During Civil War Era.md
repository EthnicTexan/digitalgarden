#### This era spans from **1861-1865**

# 1861
---
> **Secession:** Despite Sam Houston's opposition, Texas votes to secede and joins the Confederate States of America. Houston is removed from office after refusing to take an oath of allegiance to the Confederacy.

# 1862
---
> **The Battle of Galveston:** Confederate forces recapture the port of Galveston from the Union navy in a daring New Year's attack. Meanwhile, the "Great Hanging at Gainesville" sees 40 Unionists executed.


# 1863
---
> **The Battle of Sabine Pass:** A small unit of Confederate soldiers (The Davis Guards) successfully repels a Union invasion force, preventing Northern troops from occupying East Texas.

# 1864
---
> **The Red River Campaign:** Texas troops participate in battles in Louisiana to block a Union advance aimed at invading Texas from the East. On the home front, Texas serves as the "Storehouse of the Confederacy."


# 1865
---
**Juneteenth and the End of the War:** The last battle of the Civil War is fought at **Palmito Ranch** (near Brownsville) _after_ the war had officially ended. On June 19, General Gordon Granger arrives in Galveston to announce that all slaves are free.
